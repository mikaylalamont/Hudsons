ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was opened 2 years ago at Mueller College. The cafe gives a space to the year 12's as well as teachers and members of the community to come together over good food and coffee. The cafe also gives the oppitunity to hospitality students to complete their apprentiships and traineeships within the cafe.

Opening hours: Monday - Friday from 8am to 4pm.

Address: Morris road, Rothwell
